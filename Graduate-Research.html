Graduate Research Planned Sites

By Jenna Travers for GEOG 490: Web Mapping

I'm starting my Masters in Marine Resource Management in the fall at Oregon State University, and I'm currently to planning my thesis research with my advisor. I plan to study climate change driven bioinvasions of Pacific salmon species toward the poles as their current mid-latitude habitat grows too warm for the cold-water fish. I created this website to visualize my research plan and sites spatially. The first map is an overview of the research sites and my home at Oregon State, followed by maps of the two research sites. Click on the markers for more information!
